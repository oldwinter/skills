# Rubric (Score 0/1/2)

Score each item 0/1/2:
- 0 = missing / unusable
- 1 = present but incomplete
- 2 = clear, executable, reusable

Suggested passing bar: **>= 16/20**.

## 1) Discovery & triggering (0–2)
- Description + scope match real user phrasing (“set OKRs”, “quarterly goals”, “key results”, “goal setting”).

## 2) Boundaries (0–2)
- “When NOT to use” is explicit, including when to do strategy/North Star work first.

## 3) Input contract (0–2)
- Minimum inputs are listed and missing-info strategy is clear (ask up to 5, then assume).

## 4) Output contract (0–2)
- Deliverables are explicit, ordered, and checkable.

## 5) Alignment quality (0–2)
- Each objective is one step away from the company goal and the linkage is documented.

## 6) Objective quality (0–2)
- Objectives are outcome-oriented, few in number, and change weekly prioritization.

## 7) KR quality (0–2)
- KRs are measurable with definitions/baselines/targets/owners; not a pile of vanity metrics.

## 8) Anti-gaming + guardrails (0–2)
- Ratio risks are handled; per-KR failure modes and guardrails exist.

## 9) Systems + cadence (0–2)
- “Default-on” systems/habits are specified; review/grading cadence creates a learning loop.

## 10) Shareability (0–2)
- Pack can be shared with stakeholders as-is; assumptions and next steps are explicit.
