# Rubric (0–2 scoring)

Score each category 0/1/2:
- 0 = missing / unusable
- 1 = present but incomplete
- 2 = clear, actionable, and reusable

Suggested passing bar: **>= 16/20**.

## 1) Triggering + scope (0–2)
- Realistic trigger phrases and clear “when to use / not use”.

## 2) Boundary + success measures (0–2)
- Boundary is actionable; metrics and guardrails are explicit.

## 3) Actors + incentives realism (0–2)
- Incentives/constraints/power dynamics are plausible and complete enough.

## 4) System map quality (0–2)
- Variables are concrete; causal links are directional and testable.

## 5) Feedback loops + delays (0–2)
- Loops are identified with “so what”; key delays are captured.

## 6) Second-/third-order effects (0–2)
- Effects ledger is specific, includes winners/losers, mitigations, and guardrails.

## 7) Leverage points + interventions (0–2)
- Interventions are tied to the system map, owned, sequenced, and measurable.

## 8) System-build opportunity (0–2)
- Converts recurring pain into a concrete process/tool/automation with a first step.

## 9) Decision usability (0–2)
- Produces a decision-ready narrative with explicit trade-offs and uncertainties.

## 10) Safety + robustness (0–2)
- Avoids secrets; includes rollback/stop conditions; avoids overconfidence where data is missing.
