# Rubric (Startup Pivoting)

Score each item 0/1/2:
- 0 = missing / unusable
- 1 = present but incomplete
- 2 = clear, executable, reusable

Suggested passing bar: **>= 16/20**.

## 1) Decision framing (0–2)
- Owner + date + binary decision are explicit.

## 2) Evidence quality (0–2)
- Evidence is inventoried; gaps are labeled; assumptions are explicit.

## 3) Diagnosis clarity (0–2)
- “What’s stuck” is specific (not generic); bottlenecks are ranked.

## 4) Exhaustion check integrity (0–2)
- Non-pivot levers are evaluated fairly and time-boxed where appropriate.

## 5) Option set quality (0–2)
- 4–8 distinct options; includes at least one 200% pivot; each has “what must be true”.

## 6) Thesis quality (0–2)
- Pivot thesis is falsifiable and internally consistent (persona/problem/promise/distribution).

## 7) Metrics and gates (0–2)
- North Star + leading indicators + guardrails + kill criteria are concrete and dated.

## 8) Validation plan strength (0–2)
- Contains a hard-truth test; learning goals map to decisions.

## 9) Execution realism (0–2)
- Plan fits runway and team capacity; includes cut list and ownership.

## 10) Shareability + safety (0–2)
- Memo is readable and decision-ready; includes risks/open questions/next steps; human checkpoint included.
