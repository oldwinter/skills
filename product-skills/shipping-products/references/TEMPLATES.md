### 2. Rollout plan
- **Release type:** internal | beta | GA | phased | silent

- **Release notes:** (draft)
- **Docs/help center updates:** (what/owner/date)
- **Sales/support enablement:** (what’s new, FAQs, talk track)

### 6. Launch day runbook
- **Roles:** (incident lead, comms lead, eng lead, support lead)

- **Escalation path:** (who, when, how)

- **What data we will review:**

### 8. Risks / Open questions / Next steps
- **Risks:** (with likelihood/impact + mitigations)
- **Open questions:** (that could change ship decision)
- **Next steps:** (owner + date)
