# Scoring Rubric (0/1/2)

Score each item:
- 0 = missing / unusable
- 1 = present but incomplete
- 2 = clear, executable, reusable

Suggested passing bar: **≥ 16/20**.

## 1) Decision clarity (0–2)
- The decision statement, horizon, and audience are explicit.

## 2) Problem and “why now” (0–2)
- Problem is user-centered with evidence; “why now” is concrete.

## 3) Differentiation (0–2)
- Differentiation is defensible and not model/vendor-driven.

## 4) Strategy choices and non-goals (0–2)
- Clear choices + 3+ non-goals; assumptions are labeled.

## 5) Use-case portfolio quality (0–2)
- Multiple candidates considered; top bets are measurable and workflow-anchored.

## 6) Autonomy policy quality (0–2)
- Autonomy boundaries are explicit; action-taking has approvals, permissions, audit, rollback.

## 7) Eval + measurement plan (0–2)
- Includes offline + online evaluation, plus utility and risk signals.

## 8) Data + governance readiness (0–2)
- Data sources and prohibited data are explicit; governance constraints acknowledged.

## 9) Empirical learning plan quality (0–2)
- Experiments are timeboxed with decision rules, metrics, and owners.

## 10) Roadmap executability (0–2)
- Phased roadmap with exit criteria, owners, and risk retirement work.
