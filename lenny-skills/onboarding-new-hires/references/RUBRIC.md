# Rubric (score 1–5; 3 = acceptable)

## 1) Role clarity + success definition
**1** No concrete success definition.  
**3** 30/60/90 defined with basic deliverables and checks.  
**5** 30/60/90 + 1y/2y are crisp, measurable, and stakeholder-aligned.

## 2) Belonging + social integration
**1** No buddy/pair plan; onboarding is mostly solo reading.  
**3** Buddy + first pair exist; intros and rituals are scheduled.  
**5** Social integration is intentional, timeboxed, and covers key interfaces; isolation risks are mitigated.

## 3) Listening tour design + synthesis
**1** No structured listening tour; meetings are ad hoc.  
**3** Stakeholder map + question guide + synthesis table exist.  
**5** Coverage is complete; synthesis yields clear themes, trade-offs, and a credible focus proposal.

## 4) Working agreement (relationship design)
**1** Expectations and norms are implicit.  
**3** Basic agreement on communication, expectations, and escalation.  
**5** Decision rights, feedback cadence, and operating rhythms are explicit and documented.

## 5) Practicality + operational readiness
**1** Outputs are generic and not usable.  
**3** Plan is realistic and can be executed with moderate effort.  
**5** Plan is calendar-ready; dependencies, owners, and constraints are handled.

## 6) Risk awareness
**1** No risks or open questions.  
**3** Risks and open questions exist with some mitigations.  
**5** Risks are prioritized with specific mitigations, checkpoints, and “smallest next actions”.

## Scoring interpretation
- **24–30:** Excellent; ready to run.
- **18–23:** Good; minor gaps to address.
- **12–17:** Risky; revise before use.
- **<12:** Not usable; rebuild the pack.
