# Rubric (score the Candidate Evaluation Decision Pack)

Score each category 0/1/2:
- 0 = missing / unusable
- 1 = present but incomplete
- 2 = clear, decision-ready, evidence-based

Suggested passing bar: **>= 12/16**

## 1) Decision framing (0–2)
- Role, level, success outcomes, and decision deadline are explicit
- Signals included/excluded are stated with rationale

## 2) Criteria + bar clarity (0–2)
- 4–8 criteria with observable definitions
- Red flags are explicit and relevant

## 3) Evidence quality (0–2)
- Evidence is specific (examples, quotes, outcomes) not adjectives
- Confidence/limitations are stated

## 4) Work sample/trial quality (0–2)
- Task is job-relevant, scoped, and fairly scored
- Rubric has anchors and maps to criteria

## 5) Reference check quality (0–2)
- Reference notes include examples + calibration
- Summary yields a clear rehire signal and context

## 6) Synthesis coherence (0–2)
- Recommendation matches weighted evidence and addresses red flags
- Disconfirming evidence is included

## 7) Risks + mitigations (0–2)
- Risks are concrete and plausible
- Mitigations are actionable (onboarding, checkpoints, support)

## 8) Fairness + privacy (0–2)
- Process reduces bias and avoids “vibes”
- Output respects privacy/PII constraints
