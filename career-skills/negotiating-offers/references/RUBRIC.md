# Rubric (Score 0/1/2)

0 = missing/unusable • 1 = present but weak • 2 = clear, executable, reusable  
Suggested passing bar: **>= 12/16**

## 1) Boundaries + safety (0–2)
- 0: No boundaries; risky advice; asks for secrets
- 1: Mentions boundaries but doesn’t operationalize
- 2: Clear boundaries + safe defaults + flags legal/tax items

## 2) Inputs captured (0–2)
- 0: Missing core offer/timeline/priorities
- 1: Partial intake; assumptions unclear
- 2: Clear snapshot; assumptions labeled; 3–5 questions max at a time

## 3) Decision clarity (0–2)
- 0: No BATNA; no deadline; unclear priorities
- 1: Some clarity but conflicts remain
- 2: Must/trade/nice/no-go and BATNA are consistent and usable

## 4) Success conditions quality (0–2)
- 0: No resource/role-success asks
- 1: Asks exist but vague or not outcome-tied
- 2: Outcome-tied asks with verification signals and a hiring-manager agenda

## 5) Tradeoff matrix quality (0–2)
- 0: List of asks only
- 1: Some tradeoffs but unclear
- 2: Clear levers, priorities, rationale, and explicit trades

## 6) Ask packaging + strategy (0–2)
- 0: No A/B/C; no sequence
- 1: Packages exist but inconsistent or unrealistic
- 2: Coherent A/B/C + sequencing that matches constraints and leverage

## 7) Scripts quality (0–2)
- 0: No scripts; generic advice
- 1: Scripts exist but too long/vague
- 2: Sendable, role-appropriate scripts + follow-up recap template

## 8) Finish quality (0–2)
- 0: Missing risks/open questions/next steps
- 1: Included but generic
- 2: Specific risks/questions/next actions with dates/owners where possible
