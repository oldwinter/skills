{Name}

## 9) Day-of runbook (skeleton)

- **T-60m:** final checks (site, signup, analytics, comms)
- **T-0:** publish/send
- **T+30m:** monitor traffic + conversion + support inbox
- **T+2h:** quick recap + next actions
- **T+24h:** results snapshot + iterate

## 10) Post-launch retro prompts

- What channel surprised us (positive/negative)?
- What hook/message got repeated by others?
- Where did users drop off (traffic → signup → activation)?
- What should we do 5 more times? What should we stop?
