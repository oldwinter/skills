# Source Summary (Refound/Lenny → Skill Pack)

Source: `sources/refound/raw/content-marketing/SKILL.md`

## What was preserved (core insights → executable rules)

### 1) Demand-first SEO (Meltem Kuran)
- **Insight preserved:** If no one searches for it, SEO content won’t be found.
- **Rule in this pack:** No SEO piece is approved without a demand signal.
- **Where implemented:** `references/WORKFLOW.md` (“SEO rule”) + `references/CHECKLISTS.md`.

### 2) “Content market fit” (Camille Ricketts)
- **Insight preserved:** Content needs product-like fit with audience needs/anxieties.
- **Rule in this pack:** Start with anxieties → promises → repeatable content types.
- **Where implemented:** SKILL step 2 + `references/TEMPLATES.md` (content market fit brief).

### 3) Human voice + single channel focus (Lulu Cheng Meservey)
- **Insight preserved:** Trust comes from a human voice; pick a channel that fits the spokesperson.
- **Rule in this pack:** Choose one primary channel for 4–8 weeks; avoid corporate ghost tone.
- **Where implemented:** SKILL step 4 + voice checklist in `references/CHECKLISTS.md`.

### 4) Blog post > press release (Emilie Gerber)
- **Insight preserved:** A blog post can replace a formulaic press release while staying informative.
- **Rule in this pack:** Announcements default to a first-person blog post with all news elements.
- **Where implemented:** SKILL step 7 + `references/TEMPLATES.md` announcement template.

### 5) AI-assisted (not AI-only) content (Ethan Smith)
- **Insight preserved:** Use AI as an assistant with human oversight; avoid “model-collapse” sameness.
- **Rule in this pack:** AI can draft/structure; humans must add information gain and verify facts/claims.
- **Where implemented:** SKILL step 7 + `references/TEMPLATES.md` AI SOP + `references/CHECKLISTS.md`.
