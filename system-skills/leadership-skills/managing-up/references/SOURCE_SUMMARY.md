## Preserved insights → rules/checks

### 1) Proactive partnership mindset (Wes Kao)
- **Rule:** Treat managing your manager as a proactive partnership that builds trust and opportunity.
- **Check:** The pack includes a durable comms cadence and a manager profile with explicit “what I will do differently” commitments.

### 2) Set boundaries when leader pace is unrealistic (Chip Conley)
- **Rule:** Push back on unreasonable expectations by anchoring on outcomes and proposing alternatives.
- **Check:** The pack includes boundary scripts and a working agreement (cadence, expectations, escalation triggers).

### 3) Leverage leaders to bulldoze blockers (Boz)
- **Rule:** Convert blockers into concrete actions a leader can take; do not try to solve everything alone.
- **Check:** The pack includes an escalation trigger list and an ask ladder with time-bounded, actionable requests.

### 4) Communicate context + trade-offs upward (Casey Winters)
- **Rule:** Escalate early and include explicit trade-offs so leadership can evaluate decisions with proper context.
- **Check:** The pack includes an exec-ready weekly update format and a decision/trade-off memo template.

### 5) Earn a seat at the table via business perspective (Christopher Miller)
- **Rule:** Contribute to higher-level strategy discussions before being formally invited by producing crisp POVs and pre-reads.
- **Check:** The pack includes a 30-day influence loop and pre-wiring guidance for exec forums.
