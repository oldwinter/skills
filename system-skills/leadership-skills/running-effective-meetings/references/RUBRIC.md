# RUBRIC (Definition of Done)

Score each dimension 0/1/2:
- 0 = missing / unclear
- 1 = present but weak
- 2 = clear, executable, and likely to work

Suggested passing bar: **≥ 16/20**.

## 1) Objective clarity (0–2)
- The meeting objective states what changes after the meeting.

## 2) Right meeting format (0–2)
- The chosen format matches the goal (operational vs strategic vs decision vs workshop) and avoids status-in-disguise meetings.

## 3) Discover/Discuss/Decide separation (0–2)
- Pre-read covers discovery; live meeting is discussion-first; decisions are explicitly recorded.

## 4) Pre-read quality (0–2)
- Includes context, options, and open questions; not a dump of raw info.

## 5) Agenda quality (0–2)
- Timed, limited topics, explicit outputs per segment, preserves discussion time.

## 6) Facilitation readiness (0–2)
- Roles assigned; script includes priming + prompts + “if stuck” branches.

## 7) Decision/action capture (0–2)
- Decision log and action items are filled with owners and due dates.

## 8) Close-out alignment (0–2)
- Meeting ends with explicit answers to: decide / do / who-else-needs-to-know.

## 9) Follow-up quality (0–2)
- Follow-up message is sent quickly and updates the relevant trackers.

## 10) Hygiene improvements (0–2)
- Includes at least one change to reduce future meeting waste (split/kill/shorten/off-ramp).
