## Core insights preserved

1) **Public philosophy as passive onboarding (Brandon Chu)**
- Preserved as a rule: create a **context pack / operating manual** that includes philosophy + mental models.

2) **Diagnose before treat (Deb Liu)**
- Preserved as a rule: run a structured **0–30 day listening tour** and write a **30-day state-of-the-union** before major changes.

3) **Belonging and pairing (Heidi Helfand)**
- Preserved as a rule: assign a **buddy** and a **first pair**; avoid leaving new hires alone on day one.

4) **Relationship design (Kenneth Berger)**
- Preserved as a rule: hold an early **relationship design conversation** and document a **working agreement**.

5) **90-day plan + longer trajectory (Lauren Ipsen)**
- Preserved as a rule: define success at **30/60/90** plus **1-year / 2-year** horizons.
