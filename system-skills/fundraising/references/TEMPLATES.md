## 5) Outreach + Follow-up Scripts (templates)

### Warm intro ask (to a connector)
Subject: Intro to [Investor] for [Company]?

Hi [Name] — quick ask. We’re building **[one-liner]** and are raising a **[round]** to reach **[milestone]**.  
Would you be open to intro’ing us to **[Investor]**? If yes, here’s a short blurb you can forward:

Forwardable blurb:
> [Founder] is building [one-liner]. Proof: [1 metric/proxy]. They’re raising [amount range] to reach [milestone] by [date]. Would love 20 minutes.

### Cold email (if needed)
Subject: [Outcome] for [ICP] — raising [round]

Hi [Investor] — I’m [Name], cofounder of [Company]. We help **[ICP]** achieve **[outcome]**.  
In the last [time period], we’ve seen **[proof]**. We’re raising a **[round]** to reach **[milestone]**.

Are you open to a 20-minute intro call next week?

### Post-meeting follow-up (within 24h)
Subject: Thanks — [Company] recap + next steps

Thanks for the time today. Quick recap:
- What we do: [one-liner]
- Proof: [metric/proxy]
- Raise: [amount range] to reach [milestone] by [date]

Next step: [CTA — partner meeting / deeper dive / send deck + metrics].  
Attached/linked: [deck/doc/demo].
