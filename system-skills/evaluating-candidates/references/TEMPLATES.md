## 5) Reference check outreach + script

### Outreach message (edit)
Hi <Name> — I’m considering <Candidate> for a <Role> role. With <Candidate>’s permission, I’d love 20 minutes to learn about working together (strengths, growth areas, and fit for this type of role). Are you open to a quick call?

### Script (ask for examples)
1) In what context did you work with <Candidate> and for how long?
2) What were they responsible for? What did “great” look like in that environment?
3) What are their biggest strengths? (Ask for 1–2 concrete examples.)
4) Where do they struggle or need support? (Examples + patterns.)
5) How do they handle feedback and disagreement? Any defensiveness? (Examples.)
6) How is their execution under ambiguity / high velocity? (Examples.)
7) Compared to others at this level, where do they sit? (Top 10%? middle? why?)
8) Would you hire them again? In what role/context? What would you avoid?
9) Anything I should have asked that I didn’t?
