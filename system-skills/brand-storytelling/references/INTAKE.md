# Intake (copy/paste questions)

Ask up to **5** questions at a time. If answers aren’t available, proceed with explicit assumptions and label TBDs.

## A) Goal + audience
1) What’s the primary goal? (pick one)  
   - awareness / trust / conversion / fundraising / recruiting / repositioning
2) Who is the primary audience? (pick one primary)  
   - customers / investors / partners / press / candidates / community
3) Where will this story be used first? (pick 1–2)  
   - website (home/about) / pitch deck / demo / keynote / podcast / social
4) What should the audience believe/do after the story? (1 sentence CTA)

## B) Desired brand perception
5) Today, people think we are: <adjectives> (if known)  
6) We want people to think we are: <3–5 adjectives>  
7) Brand personality/voice: (pick 2–4)  
   - bold, calm, contrarian, playful, premium, practical, nerdy, rebellious, warm, minimalist

## C) Story raw material (truth only)
8) What’s the “before” state? (problem, frustration, constraint)  
9) What was the turning point or realization? (a specific moment/event)  
10) What changed after? (new belief, new approach, new product)  
11) What was hard? (tradeoffs, risks, failure, doubt)  
12) What proof do we have? (metrics, customer quotes, credibility, screenshots, artifacts)

## D) Product + positioning snapshot (lightweight)
13) What do you do (1 sentence) and who is it for?  
14) What alternative are you replacing? (status quo, competitor, workaround)  
15) What’s meaningfully different about your approach?

## E) Constraints + guardrails
16) Any compliance/claims rules? Any taboo words or claims to avoid?  
17) What can’t be shared publicly? (customer names, roadmap, revenue, security details)  
18) Any tone constraints? (e.g., “no hype”, “no profanity”, “humble confidence”)

## F) “Build in public” feasibility (optional)
19) Who can post? (founder only vs team) Which platforms?  
20) What cadence is realistic? (daily/weekly) Any content formats you prefer?  
21) What topics are off-limits? What approval process exists (if any)?
