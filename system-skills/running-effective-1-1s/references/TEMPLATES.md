## 3) 1:1 Agenda “menu” (pick 1–3 per meeting)

- “What’s the hardest thing right now?”
- “What options have you tried?”
- “What decision do you need to make by when?”

- “What should I do more/less of as your manager?”

- “What skill do you want to get better at this quarter?”
- “What project would stretch you in a good way?”

Wellbeing/recovery
- “How’s your energy this week (1–10)?”
- “What are you doing for joy? Anything we should change to protect recovery?”

## 4) Coaching Conversation Script (5–10 minutes)
1) Outcome: “What outcome do you want?”  
2) Options: “What options have you considered?”  
3) Tradeoffs: “What are the tradeoffs and risks?”  
4) Smallest step: “What’s the smallest next step this week?”  
5) Commitment: “What will you do by when? How should we check in?”

## 7) Post-crisis Listening Session (60 min)

Goal: help the person feel heard; do not turn this into status.

1) Opening (2 min)
- “I’d like to know your thoughts and feelings about what happened.”

2) Listening (40–50 min)
- Reflect back what you hear; ask clarifying questions; avoid defending/explaining.

3) Support + clarity (8–15 min)
- “What support do you need from me this week?”
- “What would help you feel safe and effective in the short term?”

4) Close (2 min)
- Summarize what you heard; agree on any small next steps.

## 8) Skip-level Template (30–45 min)

### Notes and follow-up rules

- Do not make commitments that conflict with the direct manager.
