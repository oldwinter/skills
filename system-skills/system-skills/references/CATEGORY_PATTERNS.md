# Category → Work Patterns

Use this mapping to decide what kind of “execution contract” to generate.

## Product Management
Common patterns:
- Doc generator (PRD/spec/strategy/brief)
- Research & synthesis (interviews, surveys, feedback analysis)
- Prioritization & roadmap (scoring, trade-offs, sequencing)
- Launch/rollout planning

## Hiring & Teams
Common patterns:
- Process playbook (hiring loop, interview plan, scorecards)
- Coaching & feedback (1:1s, performance reviews)
- Team norms & culture (principles, rituals, escalation paths)

## Leadership
Common patterns:
- Decision frameworks (trade-offs, planning under uncertainty)
- Communication artifacts (exec updates, narratives)
- Org design and operating cadence

## AI & Technology
Common patterns:
- Build/evaluate loops (eval specs, benchmarks, failure modes)
- Prompt sets & prototype plans
- Architecture and constraints docs

## Communication
Common patterns:
- Stakeholder alignment (decision logs, pre-reads, meeting plans)
- Writing & messaging (memos, emails, narratives)

## Growth / Marketing / Sales & GTM
Common patterns:
- Messaging frameworks + copy drafts
- Funnel diagnostics + experiment plans
- Qualification scripts + sales playbooks

## Engineering / Design
Common patterns:
- Technical strategy docs (roadmaps, infra plans)
- Design review playbooks + design system guidance
