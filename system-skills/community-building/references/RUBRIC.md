# Rubric (1–5)

Score the Community Building Pack. A “ship-ready” pack is typically **≥ 22/30** with no category below 3.

## 1) Goal + audience fit (1–5)
- **1:** Goal/ICP are unclear; no timebox or metric.
- **3:** Goal/ICP/timebox/metrics exist; some gaps remain.
- **5:** Primary goal is crisp, timeboxed, and measurable; ICP and member criteria are explicit.

## 2) Movement + value exchange strength (1–5)
- **1:** Generic positioning; unclear why anyone joins.
- **3:** Thesis exists; value exchange is partially defined.
- **5:** Strong, non-generic thesis with clear identity and explicit give/get exchange.

## 3) Platform strategy realism (1–5)
- **1:** Platform chosen by preference/trend; no outpost plan.
- **3:** Home base exists; outposts are listed.
- **5:** “Go where they are” approach with owners, onboarding flow, and a feasible home base + outposts plan.

## 4) Launch + programming executability (1–5)
- **1:** Vague launch; no calendar or owners.
- **3:** Basic 30/60/90 and calendar exist.
- **5:** Concrete seeding plan + scripts + 4–8 week rituals calendar with goals, owners, and member-generated content.

## 5) Governance + safety (1–5)
- **1:** No moderation plan; unsafe/unscalable.
- **3:** Rules exist; enforcement is light.
- **5:** Clear conduct, privacy norms, escalation ladder, and moderation capacity matched to platform.

## 6) Measurement + operating cadence (1–5)
- **1:** No metrics; no review loop.
- **3:** Metrics exist; cadence is basic.
- **5:** Metrics by journey stage + weekly ops cadence + decision rules and product/feedback loop.
