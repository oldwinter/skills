## Preserved insights → rules/checks

### 1) Schedule time for your energy (Anneka Gupta)
- **Rule:** Design the week around energy windows (high/medium/low), not just availability.
- **Check:** The pack includes an **Energy-Aligned Default Week** with time blocks + buffers.

### 2) Identify “Energy Drivers” and “Energy Drains” (Bob Moesta)
- **Rule:** Track what gives energy vs drains energy and use it to redesign work, meetings, and role mix.
- **Check:** The pack includes an **Energy Log** and **Drivers & Drains Map** with levers + next actions.

### 3) Audit your calendar for zone-of-genius work (Claire Vo)
- **Rule:** Bucket time and actively reduce the bottom bucket; increase time in the top bucket.
- **Check:** The pack includes a **Calendar Energy Audit** plus a **Zone of Genius Expansion Plan** (stop/delegate/automate/defer).

### 4) Manage neurological load in remote work (Donna Lichaw)
- **Rule:** Add buffers, transitions, and optional sensory/movement supports to reduce cognitive load.
- **Check:** The pack includes a **Recovery + Transition Plan** and meeting-hygiene defaults (buffers, shorter meetings, async-first).

### 5) Use “after-action energy” as a compass (Paul Millerd)
- **Rule:** After key interactions, note energized vs sapped; use patterns to adjust commitments and scope.
- **Check:** The pack’s baseline step and templates include an after-action tracking prompt and a 2-week pilot loop.
