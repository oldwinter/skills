# Intake (copy/paste questions)

Ask up to **5** questions at a time. If answers aren’t available, proceed with explicit assumptions and label TBDs.

## A) Goal + timing
1) What’s the announcement type? (launch / funding / partnership / feature / report / hiring / other)  
2) What’s the desired publish window? Any embargo constraints?  
3) What does success look like? (quantity + quality; audience reached; downstream outcomes)

## B) Audience + angles
4) Who is the primary audience you want to reach, and what do they care about?  
5) What’s the “new” part (what changed)? Why now?  
6) What are 2–3 credible specifics/proof points we can share? (metrics, customers, demo, screenshots)

## C) Targets + relationships
7) Any must-have outlets/reporters? Any to avoid?  
8) Any existing relationships with journalists? Any warm intros available?  
9) Any prior coverage examples we want to emulate? (links)

## D) Spokesperson + materials
10) Who can speak on the record? Availability?  
11) What assets exist today? (press release, blog post, deck, product images, logos, customer quotes)  
12) Any sensitive topics to anticipate? (competitors, pricing, layoffs, security, roadmap)

## E) Constraints + approvals
13) What cannot be shared publicly? (customer names, revenue, roadmap, security details)  
14) Any legal/compliance constraints or required reviewers?  
15) Any tone constraints? (no hype, technical depth, founder voice, etc.)
